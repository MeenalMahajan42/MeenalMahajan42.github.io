About Me

Accomplished Chemistry Educator & Digital Instructor

With over 15 years of experience in CBSE senior secondary education, I am passionate about making Chemistry accessible, engaging, and meaningful for every student. My journey spans traditional classroom teaching, examination evaluation at the national level, and modern digital content creation.

As an experienced AISSCE Evaluator and Assistant Head Examiner, I bring deep expertise in curriculum design, assessment strategies, and examination cell operations. I believe in concept-based pedagogy that not only prepares students for exams but instills a genuine love for the subject.

I've been fortunate to mentor several talented students throughout my career, including a remarkable student who achieved a perfect 100/100 in Chemistry in Class XII boards. Such moments remind me why I chose this profession — witnessing young minds flourish and reach their full potential is truly rewarding.

Currently, I'm pursuing my Ph.D. in Nanomaterials while sharing my knowledge through my YouTube channel Molecule Mentor, reaching students across India and beyond.
